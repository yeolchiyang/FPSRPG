This demon comes with 2 lava whips : 1 / hell whip idle
                                     2 / hell whip
User has to switch from one whip to another when he desires to make the demon take the whip or put it back around its neck.
animation name : 1 / groundedPutBackWhip : hell whip visible from frame 0 to frame 44, then turn off its visibility on frame 45 and turn on the visibility of the hell whip idle on frame 45.
                 
                 2 / groundedGetWhip : hell whip not visible from frame 0 to frame 9 then become visible on frame 10, hell whip idle visible from frame 0 to frame 9, then become invisible on frame 10
